Mohamed Ilyes Lakhal

CVSSP, University of Surrey. Office 10 BA 00.

Mohamed Lakhal is a Research Fellow in Computer Vision and Deep Learning at the Centre for Vision, Speech and Signal Processing (CVSSP) at the University Of Surrey. He holds a PhD in computer science from Queen Mary University of London, , affiliated with the Centre for Intelligent Sensing (CIS). His research interests are in image and video synthesis with application to multi-view setting, and Sign Language Production.
